        Courtroom Conduct

        • You are required to act as if you were in a courtroom setting. I do not care about honorifics, and I do not care about anything that happens outside of the courtroom (excluding ex parte comms and other shenanigans).
        • Do not edit, delete, or otherwise modify messages. Instead, reply to the message you intend to correct with an asterisk and the intended conveyance of the message.
        • Unless stated otherwise, attorneys not already listed in the Trello card for a case must file a notice of appearance in #filing-center.
        • Try to keep banter and unnecessary shitposting to a minimum, preferably none at all.
        • All case under my docket are considered to be in session unless otherwise stated.
        • By taking part in a proceeding under my category, it is assumed that you have read these rules and agreed to them. These rules and any others conveyed in a case channel are considered orders by a judicial officer. Contravention of these rules can make you liable to 1 M.S.C. ยง 1501 "Contempt of Court," with a fine of up to $7,500.00 or five (5) minutes in the county jail.
